# Privacy Policy — Gmail Sender Grouper

Last updated: April 2026

## Data We Collect
- **Email metadata**: We read your Gmail emails solely to group 
  them by sender. This data never leaves your device.
- **License key**: We store your payment ID locally on your 
  device using Chrome storage to verify your purchase.
- **Google account**: We use your Google identity only to 
  authenticate access to your Gmail data.

## Data We Do NOT Collect
- We do not store your emails on any server
- We do not sell or share your data with anyone
- We do not track your browsing activity

## Third Party Services
- Razorpay (Indian payments) — handles payment processing
- Dodo Payments (International) — handles payment processing
- Vercel — hosts our license verification function
