Gmail Sender Grouper (GSG)

Organize your Gmail inbox with sender-based grouping.

Gmail Sender Grouper is a Chrome extension that helps you organize your inbox by grouping emails based on sender.

Features

  • Group emails by sender instantly
  • Clean and simple interface
  • Secure login using Google

How it works

After logging in with your Google account, the extension reads your Gmail metadata to group emails locally on your device. No data is stored externally.
